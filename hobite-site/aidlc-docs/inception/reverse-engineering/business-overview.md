# Business Overview

## Business Description

Hobite Capital is a research-first investing website. It publishes long-form research, portfolio-oriented platform pages, a newsletter entry point, an operation log, and a lightweight browser-based blog/admin experience.

The proposed elimination prediction feature expands the existing platform area from static investment-framework content into an interactive user-facing tool. The tool lets users complete a knockout bracket, identify a predicted champion, and generate a shareable poster image.

## Business Transactions

- **Read research content**: Users browse equity research pages and reports.
- **View platform positioning**: Users review portfolio strategy and framework pages.
- **Manage local blog content**: Admin page stores local test posts in browser storage.
- **Create elimination prediction**: Proposed feature where users configure participants and select winners.
- **Generate prediction poster**: Proposed feature where a completed prediction becomes a branded image.
- **Share prediction**: Proposed feature where users download or share the generated image.

## Business Dictionary

- **Participant**: A team, person, company, stock, or entity competing in the elimination bracket.
- **Matchup**: A head-to-head comparison between two participants.
- **Round**: A stage in the elimination bracket.
- **Champion**: The final predicted winner.
- **Share image**: A generated poster intended for download and social distribution.
- **Event details**: Title, final date, final time, and stadium or venue metadata used in the poster.

## Component Level Business Descriptions

### App Shell

- **Purpose**: Provides site navigation, metadata, and common layout.
- **Responsibilities**: Header, footer, global styling, and page composition.

### Research Pages

- **Purpose**: Publish long-form company and market research.
- **Responsibilities**: Static analysis content, charts, and bilingual research routing.

### Platform Pages

- **Purpose**: Present investor platform concepts and future interactive tools.
- **Responsibilities**: Currently static; suitable home for elimination prediction.

### Blog And Admin Pages

- **Purpose**: Provide browser-local content management and public blog display.
- **Responsibilities**: Local post creation, editing, deletion, and public rendering.

### Proposed Elimination Prediction Tool

- **Purpose**: Let users create, visualize, export, and share elimination-stage predictions.
- **Responsibilities**: Participant entry, bracket state, winner advancement, poster generation, and social sharing.
